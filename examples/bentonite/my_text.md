Weathered biotite demonstrates superior cesium sorption, often by two orders of magnitude, compared to other clay minerals like illite, smectite, kaolinite, and halloysite . [[a973183290]]

Montmorillonite also shows high adsorption capacity, outperforming illite and kaolinite . [[ec9d65184a]] [[451becad04]]

Specific bentonite types, which contain montmorillonite, are effective: Kunipia-F bentonite is superior to SiO2 , natural Ca-bentonite has a higher capacity than Na-bentonite , and inorganic modified Na-bentonite shows high adsorption under alkaline conditions . [[1cc79421db]] [[91a1c20b01]] [[0adc4e6c6d]]

Molybdenum vanadate loaded bentonite also enhances cesium removal . [[8d56fcd23b]]

Glauconite exhibits higher sorption than bentonite , and acid-activated kaolinite significantly improves natural kaolinite's efficiency . [[32d6151f27]] [[7e3a1a3ed3]]

While zeolite shows the highest overall sorption among minerals , weathered biotite appears to be the best clay sorption material. [[32d6151f27]]
